Feasibility

A website for plant health detection is feasible due to the rising demand for digital agricultural solutions. Using machine learning and image recognition, the site can accurately identify plant diseases and offer preventive measures and treatments. The growing interest in sustainable farming practices supports a broad market reach, making this platform valuable for farmers, gardeners, and agricultural professionals.

Innovation

Integrate the ML model with IoT devices such as drones, soil sensors, and cameras that can continuously monitor crops and automatically send data to the model for analysis. Automation of data collection and analysis can significantly reduce manual labor and improve the accuracy of disease detection by capturing data from multiple angles and under different conditions.

Business Model

The plant health detection website combines social impact with revenue generation. It provides free basic diagnostics and educational resources to support home gardeners and small farmers, promoting sustainability and food security. Premium features offer advanced diagnostics for a fee, while partnerships with agricultural organizations fund research and support underserved communities. A portion of profits is dedicated to global agricultural education and sustainable farming initiatives, ensuring accessibility and positive societal impact.
